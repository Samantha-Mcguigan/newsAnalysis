National Public Radio (NPR) is a non-profit and publicly funded media group that started as a news and cultural information radio broadcast. NPR is comprised of news reports and specialty segments like interviews, music, and educational content developed from a vast array of different content genres. The company has been able to share their content with other broadcasters and content creators, allowing for wider reach with radio, television, and internet cooperation.
