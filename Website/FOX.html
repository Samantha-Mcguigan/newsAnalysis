Fox News Channel is the creation of the media mogul Rupert Murdoch. Meant to be a competitor for other news networks at the time, FOX quickly diversified and grew rapidly, becoming one of the most popular news channels in the United States. FOX has a large focus on late night programing, centered on shows driven by discussion and recap of new events.
